Site Name

Name: Math Calc

The name Math Calc comes from the main purpose of the site which is educative. Users can access the site as they solve math problems to help them confirm their answers. Thus being called math Calc.

Availability: mathcal.com

Site Purpose

My chosen project topic is a website about mathematics. It will be a site that can calculate answers for various equations like quadratic equations, conversion from Fahrenheit to Celsius, from pounds to kgs etc.

The purpose of the site is to serve as a Math learning hub. It will contain mathematical formulas and equations.

Scenarios

The following are questions a visitor might ask when visiting the site:

  • Scenario 1: What is the formula for quadratic equations?
  • Scenario 2: How do I calculate for percentage?
  • Scenario 3: Are there any formulas for conversion techniques?
  • Scenario 4: Are the answers on this site accurate?
